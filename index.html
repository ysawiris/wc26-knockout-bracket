    How It Works

    The pool, in four steps

    1. 1
      Set the order. The 12 teams snake-draft in last-season finish order. Flip best-first / worst-first on the Draft Order tab.
    2. 2
      Draft your two. Snake draft means each team grabs one stronger and one weaker of the 32 Round-of-32 countries — 24 picks total.
    3. 3
      Advance to score. Every round your country wins is worth points — R32 3, R16 5, QF 8, SF 13, Final 21 — plus 0.1 per goal.
    4. 4
      Most points wins. Ties break on advancement points, then draft slot. Cards and fouls are shown but never count.

    World Cup 2026 — the knockout

    The biggest World Cup ever: 48 teams, three hosts (United States, Mexico, Canada), 104 matches. After the group stage, 32 teams reach the brand-new Round of 32 — and that's where our pool lives.

    The bracket is single-elimination: R32 → R16 → quarterfinals → semifinals → final, 31 matches in all. Top seeds can only meet in the final (1v32, 2v31, …). The final is July 19 at MetLife Stadium, New York / New Jersey.

    For the pool, only the knockout counts. You own two countries; you score every round they survive plus a small bonus for the goals they score. The further they go, the more you win.
